# Frequently Asked Questions

## How much does Aqar cost?
We offer flexible plans based on the number of properties you manage. Contact us on WhatsApp (+256 784 464178) or email info@aqarproperty.co.uk for a personalised quote.

## Do I need to install anything?
No. Aqar is fully cloud-based. You access it through your web browser on any device — phone, tablet, or computer. No downloads, no installations.

## Can I try it before I commit?
Yes. We have a live demo at property.chwezi.co.za where you can explore the full system without signing up.

## Is my data safe?
Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. Our servers are protected by firewalls, and we provide easy encrypted backup options. You own your data.

## Can I manage multiple properties?
Yes. Aqar is designed for managing portfolios of any size — from a single building to hundreds of properties across different locations.

## Does it work on mobile?
Yes. Aqar works on any device with a web browser. The interface is responsive and works well on phones, tablets, and desktop computers.

## Can tenants access the system?
Tenants can receive automated rent reminders and submit maintenance requests through the system. Communication happens within the platform.

## What if I need help getting started?
We provide hands-on onboarding support. Contact us on WhatsApp and we will walk you through setup and help you import your existing data.

## Which countries do you serve?
We serve landlords and property managers in East Africa and worldwide. The system is accessible from anywhere with an internet connection.
