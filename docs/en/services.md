# Services (Feature Modules)

## 1. Rent Collection & Reconciliation
Automated rent tracking with reconciliation that matches payments to tenants. No more spreadsheet headaches.
- Automated rent reminders sent to tenants
- Payment reconciliation — match deposits to units automatically
- Track arrears and partial payments
- Management fee calculation and landlord remittance reports

## 2. Tenant Management & Communication
Centralised tenant database with built-in messaging. Know every tenant, track every conversation.
- Centralised tenant profiles with contact details, lease history, and notes
- Inbuilt messaging system — communicate without leaving the platform
- Record incidents and complaints with timestamps
- Tenant onboarding and offboarding workflows

## 3. Maintenance Tracking
Log maintenance requests, assign tasks, track resolution. Tenants stay informed, nothing falls through the cracks.
- Tenants submit maintenance requests through the system
- Assign tasks to maintenance staff or contractors
- Track status from reported to resolved
- Maintenance cost tracking per property and unit

## 4. Financial Reports & Landlord Remittances
Crystal-clear financial visibility. Income, expenses, management fees, and landlord payouts — all in one place.
- Customisable financial reports (income, expenses, profit/loss)
- Automated management fee calculations
- Landlord remittance statements generated automatically
- Expense tracking per property, per unit, per category

## 5. Lease Agreement Management
Every lease tracked, every renewal flagged. Full history from day one.
- Digital lease agreement records
- Lease expiry alerts and renewal tracking
- Complete lease history per tenant
- Terms and conditions documentation

## 6. Mobile Access & Security
Manage properties from anywhere. Your data is encrypted and backed up.
- Cloud-based — accessible on any device with a browser
- No installation required
- Data encryption in transit and at rest
- Server firewall protection
- Easy encrypted backups
- Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
