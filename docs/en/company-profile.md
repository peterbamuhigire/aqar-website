## What We Do
Aqar is cloud-based property management software designed for landlords and property managers. It handles rent collection, tenant management, maintenance tracking, financial reporting, lease management, and mobile access. No installation required — accessible from any device with a browser.

## Who We Serve
Landlords and property management companies in East Africa and worldwide who want to replace paperwork and manual processes with a streamlined digital system.
