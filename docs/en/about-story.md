# About — The Aqar Story

## The Problem We Saw

Property managers across East Africa were drowning in paperwork. Rent records in exercise books. Tenant complaints on sticky notes. Financial reports cobbled together in spreadsheets at month-end. Landlords calling every week asking "where's my money?" and getting vague answers.

The tools that existed were built for Western markets — priced in dollars, designed for different regulations, and ignorant of how property management actually works in Kampala, Nairobi, or Dar es Salaam.

## What We Built

Aqar is property management software built in Africa, for Africa. It replaces the exercise books, the spreadsheets, and the phone calls with one clear system. Rent collection, tenant management, maintenance tracking, financial reports — all accessible from a phone or laptop, anywhere.

No installation. No complex setup. Log in and start managing.
